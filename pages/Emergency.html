Instructions on When to Call Each Type of Emergency Service:

Follow these guidelines to know when to call each type of emergency service:

  • Medical Facilities: Call for medical emergencies such as severe injuries, life-threatening conditions, or medical assistance.
  • Emergency Services: Call for immediate assistance in life-threatening situations or when there is a need for police, fire, or ambulance services.
