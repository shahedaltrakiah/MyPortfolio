Get prepared for emergencies

our simple tips

Bleeding

First aid for bleeding includes applying pressure to the wound with a clean cloth or bandage, elevating the affected limb, and applying a tourniquet if necessary.

Burns

First aid for burns includes cooling the affected area with water, covering the burn with a clean, dry cloth, and taking pain relief medication if necessary.

Choking

First aid for choking involves performing the Heimlich maneuver or back blows and abdominal thrusts to dislodge the object blocking the airway.

Heart attack

First aid for a heart attack includes calling emergency services immediately, administering aspirin if the person is able to swallow, and performing CPR if necessary.

Seizures

First aid for seizures involves ensuring the person is in a safe place, cushioning their head, and loosening any tight clothing around their neck.

Shock

First aid for shock includes laying the person flat, elevating their legs, and covering them with a blanket to keep them warm.
